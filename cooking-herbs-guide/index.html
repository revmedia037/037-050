Cooking Herbs Guide

Fresh herbs elevate dishes from ordinary to extraordinary with minimal effort. Basil pairs perfectly with tomatoes in Italian and Mediterranean cuisine. Cilantro adds bright flavor to Mexican, Indian, and Asian dishes. Rosemary's piney aroma enhances roasted meats and potatoes. Mint refreshes both sweet desserts and savory Middle Eastern dishes. Thyme works well in slow-cooked stews and French cooking. Growing your own herbs ensures freshness and saves money.
